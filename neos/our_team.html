ABOUT US:
NEOS Advisory was established to provide strategy, management and transaction advisory services, as well as capability building, focusing on international development, government and private clientele. It is a newly organized entity which was established by means of a divestiture from Tetra Tech International Inc. – Tetra Tech’s management consulting arm focusing on energy and infrastructure.
Dean S. White
Managing Partner
Dean specializes in sector reform, strategy, regulation and utility operations. He has designed and managed comprehensive energy sector reform programs such as restructuring, regulatory reform and market rule development, as well as large-scale utility performance improvement efforts. He has directed or managed large-scale energy and water reform and strategy projects in 30 countries including within Southeast Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Africa and South America. Dean has also served as the CEO of a electricity distribution utility, chaired a wholesale electricity market organization, and was executive director of several hydropower stations. Prior to Neos, Dean was leading the international management consulting arm of Tetra Tech Inc., and earlier he was a Senior Partner at PA Consulting Group responsible for the firm’s emerging markets practice. Dean studied business in University of California and public policy at Harvard University in the United States.
